# 使用、风险与非商业说明

本文件说明 nihaisha skill 的用途边界、医疗风险、版权边界和非商业使用限制。它是项目说明的一部分；如与 README 其他表述存在差异，以更保守、更安全的解释为准。

## 项目用途

本项目用于：

- 倪海厦课程学习、复习和主题检索；
- 伤寒、金匮、针灸、本草、内经等课程内容的结构化整理；
- 根据课程校对 PDF 和相关古籍资料核对术语、方名、穴名、药名和经典引用；
- 帮助学习者建立课程脉络、方证对比、穴位检索和出处追溯。

本项目不用于：

- 医疗诊断、辨病、辨证或治疗决策；
- 开方、抓药、购药、剂量判断、煎服方法或停换药建议；
- 针灸、艾灸、放血、刺络等实际操作指导；
- 替代线下医师、药师或其他合格医疗专业人员。

## 内容观点与资料属性

本项目整理的课程、文章、视频、讲稿、截图、PDF 摘录和古籍索引，主要用于学习和查证。原课程、文章或视频中的观点、经验和医案表述，属于相应讲授者、作者或原资料来源，不代表本项目提供医学判断、治疗建议或价值引导。

课程或文章中出现的治疗方式、方药、针灸操作、剂量、加减和医案处理，往往是在具体病人、具体时点、专业医师辨证和现场判断之后使用的，不具备普遍适用性。学习者不应脱离病人实际情况自行模仿；如确需参考，应在线下合格医师指导下进行。如出现任何不适或病情变化，应及时就医。

## 医疗风险

中医方药和针灸操作并不是无风险的学习材料。经方、中药、针灸和艾灸的实际使用涉及辨证、剂量、炮制、配伍、禁忌、体质、年龄、妊娠状态、基础病、合并用药和病程变化等因素。理解错误、剂量错误、药物错用或延误就医，都可能造成严重后果。

尤其涉及以下情况时，不应依赖本项目内容自行处理：

- 附子、乌头等高风险药物，或四逆汤辈等温阳回逆类方剂；
- 大承气汤、抵当汤、大陷胸汤等攻下、破血、峻剂相关内容；
- 妊娠、儿童、老人、慢性基础病、肝肾功能异常、合并多种药物；
- 胸痛、呼吸困难、意识改变、持续高热、严重腹痛、脱水、出血、肿瘤或其他急危重症；
- 针刺、放血、刺络、艾灸等需要专业训练和现场判断的操作。

真实健康问题请咨询合格医师或及时就医。本项目中的任何课程整理、检索结果、方证比较和古籍引用，都不能作为自我诊断、自行开方或自行操作的依据。

# Use, Risk, and Non-Commercial Notice

This notice defines the usage boundaries, medical risks, copyright boundaries, and non-commercial-use restrictions for the nihaisha skill. It is part of the project documentation. If this notice differs from other README wording, the more conservative and safer interpretation applies.

## Intended Use

This project is intended for:

- studying, reviewing, and searching Ni Haisha course materials;
- structurally organizing course material on Shang Han Lun, Jingui, acupuncture, materia medica, Huangdi Neijing, and related topics;
- checking terminology, formula names, acupoint names, herb names, and classical citations against proofreading PDFs and related classical materials;
- helping learners build course maps, formula-pattern comparisons, acupoint lookups, and source traceability.

This project is not intended for:

- medical diagnosis, pattern diagnosis, or treatment decisions;
- prescribing formulas, purchasing herbs, dosage decisions, decoction instructions, or medication changes;
- practical instruction for acupuncture, moxibustion, bloodletting, or related procedures;
- replacing offline physicians, pharmacists, or other qualified medical professionals.

They do not mean that this project provides medical judgment, treatment advice, or endorsement of those views.

Treatment methods, formulas, acupuncture procedures, dosages, modifications, and case handling mentioned in courses or articles usually depend on a specific patient, specific timing, professional pattern identification, and on-site clinical judgment. They are not generally applicable instructions. Learners should not imitate them outside the original clinical context. If any material is to be referenced for real health issues, it should be done under the guidance of a qualified offline clinician. Seek timely medical care if discomfort or condition changes occur.

## Medical Risk

Chinese herbal formulas and acupuncture-related procedures are not risk-free study material. Real-world use of classical formulas, herbs, acupuncture, and moxibustion depends on pattern identification, dosage, processing, compatibility, contraindications, constitution, age, pregnancy status, chronic disease, concurrent medications, and disease progression. Misunderstanding, wrong dosage, wrong medicine, or delayed care can cause serious harm.

Do not rely on this project for self-management in situations involving:

- Fuzi, Wutou, or other high-risk herbs, or Sini Tang-family warming and rescue formulas;
- Da Chengqi Tang, Didang Tang, Da Xianxiong Tang, or other strong purging, blood-breaking, or drastic formulas;
- pregnancy, children, older adults, chronic illness, liver/kidney impairment, or multiple concurrent medications;
- chest pain, breathing difficulty, altered consciousness, persistent high fever, severe abdominal pain, dehydration, bleeding, tumors, or other urgent/severe conditions;
- acupuncture, bloodletting, moxibustion, or other procedures requiring professional training and on-site judgment.

For real health problems, consult a qualified clinician or seek timely medical care. Course summaries, search results, formula-pattern comparisons, and classical citations in this project must not be used as the basis for self-diagnosis, self-prescription, or self-performed procedures.
